Notes

All three Wave-1 flows are additive on the frozen Wave-0 engine — each is its own descriptor + per-flow backend router, wired via include_router. No changes to P&L, the engine, or the delivery layer.
  • Capital Gains is the only flow with a format step (PDF/Excel) — the descriptor schema's format handling, validated live (real .xlsx delivered). Financial years are computed dynamically (current + last 2).
  • Contract Notes is the selection-step flow — date range → month-grouped tap-to-get list → per-note download. Download-only (FinX has no CN email); PDFs are not PAN-protected. file_id is never exposed — mapped to opaque server-side ids.
  • Ledger mirrors P&L with a Normal/MTF (Margin 0/1) step.
  • Not exercised live: email delivery on any flow (sends a real email to the account — skipped; email paths are unit-tested + masked). MTF ledger (Margin:1) is code-complete but the discriminator is CONFIRM-pending per the API reference.
